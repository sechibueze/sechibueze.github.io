About

Samuel Chibueze is an uncommon Educator & Web/Software developer. I am passionate about child education, youth & leadership development. My mission is to radically reform these spheres by designing teaching & learning models & curriculum focused on character & capacity development that will be used in schools to produce forward-thinking, self-reliant & purpose-driven individuals who care about making their world better.

I envision a world where individuals are rising as leaders in their area of unique gift/talent, unites with others to create a robust and sustainably developed community marked by peace and progress for all.

My work engagements are targeted at 'slotting' into the hands of our most promising assets - young people with the set of skills they will need to pursue a purposeful and impactful life. I am available as volunteer in extending digital & technology skills to our communities in Nigeria.
